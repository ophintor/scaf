• Monitoring on a weekly basis planning aplications & developments that affect the following within the Avonmouth and Kingweston ward:
    • Community buildings & facilities
    • Commercial premises
    • Business premises
  • We do this because of the loss of so many local community amenities and facilities, as well as this gives our community a chance to have our say over development & applications that will negatively affect us. This is a free service and is emailed weekly to over 200 recipients.
  • Attending BCC Planning Briefing Meetings on major developments proposed for Avonmouth.
  • Support local residents and small groups, such as the Lawrence Weston Planning Group, concerned about new planning applications in their immediate area.
