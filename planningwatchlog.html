• Weekly monitoring of all planning application and developments affecting community buildings and facilities, commercial, industrial and business premises in the Avonmouth & Lawrence Weston ward. This gives us all a chance to have our say over new development and applications that may negatively affect our community. This is a free weekly service, emailed to 250+ recipients.
  • SCAF is also a member of Bristol Neighbourhood Planning Network and so receives information on a regular basis on pre planning and outline applications.

  • Support local residents concerned about new planning applications in their area and large proposed industrial plants/developments in Avonmouth
  • Facilitating public consultations, attending BCC Planning Briefing sessions on major proposed developments
  • Hosting housing developers presentations at Full Forum Meetings
