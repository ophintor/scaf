Walking Groups

Shirehampton Community Action Forum supports two seperate walking groups within Shirehampton, both for those aged over 55.

Shire Nomads

Shire Nomads run a free walking programme for those over 55 who want to meet likeminded people along with getting fitter through walking. The walks are up to 5 miles long and can last up to 3 hours, they include a mixture of town and countryside routes and are aimed at more advanced walkers.

Shire Walkie-Talkies

Shire Walkie-Talkies is a level 3 walking group in association with SCAF, Walking for Health Bristol as well as LinkAge. The walks are suitable for those over 55 who can walk for an hour without stopping and often cover between 2 to 3 miles and can last up 90 minutes. The locations of walks vary, with some being local and some being further afield.
