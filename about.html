About Us

The overall aim of SCAF is to improve the quality of life for everyone living and working in Shirehampton.
As a charity the Shirehampton Community Action Forum takes pride in helping improve the area of Shirehampton for all those who work and live there. We accomplish this by various different groups and projects, however our most recent and major project is being a lead partner within the Shire Community Plan.

Shire Door to Door Shopping Trips

Aside from the new Community Plan for Shirehampton, we also organise free weekly door to door shopping/social trips to a variety of supermarkets/shopping centres especially for Shire residents aged 60+ in a fully accessible minibus. We also put on longer “special” trips and this year we have had extended visits in the spring and summer to Weston-Super-Mare, Abergavenny and Burnham on Sea. In early December we also run special trips to Cadbury Garden Centre.
